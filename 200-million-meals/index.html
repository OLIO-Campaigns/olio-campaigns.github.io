HOW IT WORKS

How OLIO & FareShare can redistribute your surplus food

STEP 1

Prepare

Store staff notify OLIO and FareShare that they want a collection. They then prepare any unsold food to be picked up at the end of the day.

STEP 2

Collect

Trained volunteers arrive on-site when you need them to collect your food.

STEP 3

Exchange

Food ends up on tables, not in bins, and local communities see you care about making a positive impact.

STEP 4

Report

We track the impact of these collections on your brand and local community and report this data back to you.
